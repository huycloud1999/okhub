1. General Information

Known as one of the 4 longest and most dangerous mountain passes in North Vietnam, O Qui Ho pass is also one of the most breathtaking mountain passes in Vietnam. The O Qui Ho Pass is located between Lao Cai and Lai Chau province, in the northwest region of Vietnam. This pass is considered one of the most beautiful and famous passes in Vietnam, as it offers stunning views of the surrounding mountainous landscapes and a challenging ride for adventurous travelers. Riding a motorbike through this pass must be an unmissable choice for many tourists who want to experience the beauty of the region and challenge themselves.

One of the most striking features of O Quy Ho Pass is the incredible scenery that surrounds it. The pass runs through the Hoang Lien Son Mountain Range, which is home to the highest peak in Indochina (Vietnam – Laos & Cambodia), Fansipan Mountain. The pass offers a panoramic view of the stunning landscapes of the area, including towering mountains, lush valleys, and winding rivers. The beauty of this pass is enhanced by the changing colors of the foliage in autumn and the bright green hues of the rice paddies during the rainy season.
